"CORRELATION ONE: PRODUCT DESIGN CHALLENGE"

Background:

One of my favorite parts of interviewing (in my opinion) is the chance to show a company how much you can ideate, develop, and execute an idea in a short period of time. Each design challenge that I've done so far has been a unique and enriching experience - this one was no different!

This test was directed towards their Product Designer role with a focus on UX/UI. The challenge consisted of taking a gaming concept from the team that has not yet been developed, and envisioning how I could create/arrange the constraints given into a functioning product interface.

The Results:

Although you can find the full challenge with my thoughts below, this experience was certainly a great way to flex my product muscle! From fleshing out user personas to figuring out use cases and wireframing, this process allowed me to learn more about the realm of data science. I feel as though I have a pretty solid grasp of COrrelation One's branding and visual presence in the niche they have carved out in the startup world, and I'm excited at the prospect of working with them more in the future!
