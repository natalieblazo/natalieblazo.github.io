About Me

My name is Natalie and I graduated Ithaca College with a BFA in Art and minors in Art History and Honors (May 2015). I'm a first-generation Hispanic-American with a dual-citizenship from El Salvador.
I've been steeped in all traditional forms of art making and design, but I've always had a knack for traditional AND digital drawing for years.

Nearly every job and internship I've had involved graphic design and marketing aspects. I'm always looking for opportunities to learn more and strengthen my creative muscle.

I'm a current Web Design student at the Startup Institute in NYC to hone my technical and coding skills! My dream is to put my creativity to work on the web and other interfaces that people will see and use every day.

I love finding new solutions over textbook solutions and learning by doing. I am most in my element when I can get my hands dirty!
