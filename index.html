About Me

My name is Natalie.

I graduated Ithaca College with a BFA in Art and minors in Art History and Honors (May 2015). I'm a first-generation Hispanic-American with a dual citizenship from El Salvador.

I've practiced all traditional forms of art making and design, with specialties in traditional AND digital drawing for years.

I love finding new solutions over textbook solutions and learning by doing. I am most in my element when I can get my hands dirty!

Nearly every job and internship involved graphic design and marketing aspects.

I'm always looking for opportunities to learn more and strengthen my creative muscle.

I'm a Web Design alum from the Startup Institute in NYC. My dream is to put my creativity to work on the web and other interfaces that people will see and use every day.
